Design System
Apples & Oranges
A comparison framework with two opposing sides and a neutral tone. Same layout every time — only the palette flips.
01 — Schemes
Apples Side A
Dark ground, pale text. The first option — opens the pairing.
Oranges Side B
Inverted palette — navy text on a pale ground. Same layout, mirrored.
Banana Neutral
Side-less tone for summaries, agenda, or shared context.
